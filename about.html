Goto

This is a WIP language developed by FTC Team 5913 used to more easily program autonomous modes for First Tech Challenge robots. Here's what stuff does:

goto

Tells the robot where to move and how to position itself on the field. Example: goto x: 0.3 y: 0.6 heading: 90
